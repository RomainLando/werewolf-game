How to play

The game is played in rounds with each round broken up into daytime and night-time. These rounds will continue until the game ends which happens when either the villagers eradicate all the werewolves or the village has an equal number of werewolves to villagers.

Your goal will depend on your character role, but you must to act out the role you have been assigned and help your team win.

Day-Time

Each morning the actions of the night are revealed by the narrator and the remaining players must try and figure out the roles of their neightbour and vote out one of the survivors.

Night-Time

As night falls everyone must go to sleep. Special characters will be woken up one by one byt the narrator and will be prompted to cary out their special role.

Meet the characters

Depending on the amount of players, the list of characters in game will vary. The basic list involves two teams fighting for survival.

The longer list introduces more uncertanty as more sinister characters are introduced...

The Villagers

They are player characters with no abilities that seek to kill all of the werewolves.

The Werewolves

They are the team seeking to kill all the villagers. They select a villager to kill each night.

The Doctor

They are the team seeking to kill all the villagers. They select a villager to kill each night.

The Seer

They are on the villager team and can ask the narrator once per night, to reveal if one of the other players is a werewolf or not.

The Witch

They are on the villager team and can use one potion and one poison to save a villager from werewolves and kill a suspected werewolf.

The Alpha-Werewolf

They are just like the other werewolves except that they must say the word "werewolf" at least once each day.

The Jester

They are on their own team and win if they get killed by a vote during the day.

The Vigilante

They are on the villager team and can chose to kill one person each night if they suspect them of being a werewolf.

The Cupid

They are on the villager team and chose two other players to pair together, if one gets killed the other will also die.
